Requirements & Qualifications

What you need to succeed as a Medical Form Entry Specialist

Contract Requirements

  • Complete 11 slots with 2000 forms per slot
  • Maximum 500 forms can be omitted total
  • Maintain high accuracy in form completion
  • Computer with stable internet connection
  • Meet monthly completion targets

Payment & Quality Guidelines

  • Maximum rate of ₹10 per error-free form
  • 7-15 working days verification period
  • Bi-monthly payments (15th & 30th)
  • No payment for unverified submissions
  • Forms with 3+ errors are not paid
  • Contract termination if over 500 forms missed
